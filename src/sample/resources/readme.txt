Sample programs live under ../java (package ...hi.sample). They illustrate how
to construct HI SOAP clients with keystores and endpoints; replace placeholders
with values from your ADHA/Services Australia registration (see README.md
"Background" and "Using this library in another project").

These sources are not compiled by the default Maven lifecycle. Build or run them
from your IDE as part of a Maven-imported project (so generated HI types and
dependencies resolve), or put src/main/java, src/main/resources, and
target/generated-sources/wsimport on the classpath after mvn compile. See
README.md "Project layout".
